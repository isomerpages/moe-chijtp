<p>The CHIJ Concert Band stands out as one of Singapore's leading Secondary
School Bands, celebrated for its outstanding performances. An integral
part of CHIJ Secondary, the Band has received the Certificate of Distinction
at the Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation in 2015, 2017, 2019,
2021, 2023 and 2025. The Band has had the privilege of performing at prominent
venues, including the Esplanade Concert Hall during the ‘Limelight’ series
in 2014, 2016, and 2018, 2024.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/10_Band___activity_photo_1.jpg">
</div>
<p>In November 2018, they performed at the Istana for President Halimah Yacob
during the Deepavali Open House. Looking ahead, the Band will return to
the Esplanade Concert Hall on <strong>30 March 2026</strong> for another
performance. With over a hundred dedicated students, the Band enriches
the cultural life at CHIJ Secondary while instilling discipline, perseverance,
and GRIT. Our graduates excel both musically and academically, becoming
well-rounded individuals ready to contribute to society.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CCA/Performing Arts/2024_Band_3.jpg">
</div>
<p>Aligning with the school motto, <strong><em>‘Simple in Virtue, Steadfast in Duty’</em>, </strong>the
CHIJ Concert Band values commitment and hard work in its quest for musical
excellence.</p>
