<p><strong>Time</strong>: 9.00 am to 10.30 am</p>
<p><strong>Materials required</strong>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Graphite Pencils (eg. 2B, 4B, 6B)</p>
</li>
