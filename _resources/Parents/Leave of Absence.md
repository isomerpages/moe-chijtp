<p><strong>Eligibility</strong> : &nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The Leave of Absence (LOA) scheme applies to all <strong>Singapore Citizen (SC)</strong> and <strong>Permanent Resident (PR)</strong> children
studying in CHIJ Secondary.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To be granted LOA, your child must accompanying you on an overseas posting/Business/Company's
related training with the intention of re-joining the school after returning
to Singapore &nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;The Leave of Absence (LOA) Application can be submitted via the
online form below.</p>
<p><a href="https://form.gov.sg/68a7e6a2852bf11927c93d5f" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Leave of Absence (LOA) Application Form</a>
</p>
